Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Indian travelers need a visa for Vietnam?

+

Yes. Most Indian travelers require a Vietnam visa. VIETANA helps simplify the process and provides fast support, often within 24 hours depending on requirements.

Can VIETANA customize my entire trip?

+

Absolutely. We do not believe in rigid fixed tours. Every trip can be tailored around your budget, travel style, food preferences and interests.

Is Indian food available in Vietnam?

+

Yes. From North Indian and South Indian meals to vegetarian, Jain and vegan options, we help travelers feel comfortable while still experiencing authentic Vietnam. Your food comfort matters to us.

Will someone help us while we are in Vietnam?

+

Yes. Our support team is available in India and Vietnam. We provide local assistance and help when needed before and during your journey.

Do you only offer luxury travel?

+

No. We create experiences ranging from budget-friendly adventures to premium luxury journeys.

Can you arrange airport pickup and local transport?

+

Yes. Airport pickup, local transport, private cars and travel coordination can all be arranged for a smoother experience.

What if I want hidden places and not tourist spots?

+

That is one of our strengths. We focus on experiences discovered through local knowledge — hidden cafés, food streets, lesser-known destinations and unique experiences.

Can I talk in Hindi or English?

+

Yes. We support travelers in Hindi and English so communication always feels easy and familiar.
